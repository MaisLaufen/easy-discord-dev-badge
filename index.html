Easy Discord Dev Badge

Instructions

  • Step 1:
    Make sure you run the file local_server.exe. Otherwise, the bot will not work.
  • Step 2:
    Navigate to the Discord Developer Portal, and create new application. You can use any name for it.
  • Step 3:
    Go to Bot section and add new bot.
  • Step 4:
    Click on Reset Token and copy it. Don't share it with anyone! With it, your account can be stolen.
  • Step 5:
    Paste your token in the TextBox on this page. Then press Get Invite Link button.
  • Step 6:
    Create new server and enable Community on it.
  • Step 7:
    If token is correct, you will get invite link. Copy it and paste in your browser, and invite bot to your server.
  • Step 8:
    Within the server, use /hello command in any chat.
  • Step 9:
    Claim your badge here.
    It might take 24 hours before you are able to claim at all.
